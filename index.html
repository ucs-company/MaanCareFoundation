Who We Are

About MANN CARE FOUNDATION

Trusted NGO

MANN CARE FOUNDATION is a compassionate non-profit committed to transforming lives of women and children from underprivileged and marginalized communities.

Guided by empathy and driven by measurable impact, we adopt a holistic development approach that integrates nutrition, education, health, hygiene, and women empowerment to create sustainable change.

Sustainable
Community-First
Empathetic
Measurable Impact
Our Mission

Addressing fundamental needs — nutritious food, quality education, menstrual hygiene, healthcare awareness, and self-reliance for women and children.

Our Vision

An inclusive society where every woman is empowered and every child is nourished, educated, and free from preventable illness and inequality.

Project Poshan

Nutrition Support Initiative

Addressing hunger, malnutrition and food insecurity through Dry Ration Kits, Nutritious Cooked Meals, Healthy Snack Kits, and Refreshment Support for all needy individuals.

  • Dry Ration Kits
  • Nutritious Cooked Meals
  • Healthy Snack Kits
  • Refreshment Support

Project Gyaan

Education Support Initiative

Ensuring inclusive, continuous, quality education for children from marginalized communities through academic and digital learning support.

  • School Kits & Stationery
  • Digital Education Tools
  • Computer Literacy
  • Smart Classrooms

Project Sakhi

Women Empowerment Initiative

Empowering women holistically through skill development, livelihood training, hygiene support, and confidence-building at the grassroots level.

  • Skill Development Training
  • Menstrual Hygiene Support
  • Financial Literacy
  • Leadership Development

Project Swasth

Health & Hygiene Initiative

Promoting preventive healthcare, hygiene awareness, and access to essential health support — including oral health, menstrual hygiene, and medical assistance.

  • Health Awareness Sessions
  • Sanitary Pad Distribution
  • Oral Health for Children
  • Medical Case Support

Project Pashu

Animal Support Initiative

Dedicated to the care, protection, and well-being of animals — stray dogs, abandoned cattle, injured animals — with feeding drives, medical support, and community awareness.

  • Regular Feeding Drives
  • Medical & Emergency Care
  • Seasonal Care Support
  • Community Engagement

Project Paryavaran

Environment & Sustainability Initiative

Promoting environmental protection through tree plantation, cleanliness drives, and awareness programs to build a greener and healthier future for all.

  • Tree Plantation Drives

  • Plastic-Free Awareness Programs
