Nutrition Support Initiative

Project Poshan

Nourishing Lives, Restoring Hope & Building Healthier Communities

Everyone Deserves Nutritious Food

Project Poshan is a flagship humanitarian initiative of MANN Foundation dedicated to addressing hunger, malnutrition, and food insecurity among vulnerable individuals and families. Through ration support, nutritious meals, healthy snacks, and refreshment assistance, we ensure that nourishment reaches those who need it most.

Explore Impact
About The Initiative

Fighting Hunger With Compassion

Our Mission

Access to nutritious food is not a privilege—it is a fundamental human necessity. Project Poshan works to ensure that every individual receives the nourishment required to live with dignity, health, and hope.

Why It Matters

Hunger and poor nutrition affect physical health, productivity, mental well-being, and overall quality of life. By providing food support, we help families break the cycle of hardship and insecurity.
