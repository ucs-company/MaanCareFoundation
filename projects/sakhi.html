Women Empowerment Initiative

Project Sakhi

Empowering Women Through Confidence, Skills, Health & Independence

Strong Women Build Strong Communities

Project Sakhi is a holistic women empowerment initiative dedicated to strengthening confidence, self-reliance, health awareness, and economic independence among women from marginalized communities. Through skills, knowledge, and support, the project enables women to become active contributors and leaders in society.

Explore Impact
About The Initiative

Empowering Women Beyond Limitations

Our Mission

Project Sakhi works to empower women economically, socially, physically, and emotionally by providing opportunities, awareness, resources, and support systems that enable them to live with dignity and confidence.

Why It Matters

Many women face challenges such as financial dependence, limited access to opportunities, lack of health awareness, and social barriers. Project Sakhi helps overcome these challenges through a comprehensive empowerment approach.

What We Provide

Women Empowerment Support Programs

🧵

Skill Development

Training programs that help women acquire income-generating and livelihood skills.

💼

Livelihood Support

Opportunities for sustainable employment and self-employment initiatives.

🩺

Health & Hygiene Support

Access to menstrual hygiene products, personal care resources, and health essentials.

📢

Awareness Campaigns

Sessions on health, sanitation, nutrition, rights awareness, safety, and financial literacy.
